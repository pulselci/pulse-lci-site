Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

1. Who We Are

Pulse LCI is operated by White Soap LLC, an Ohio limited liability company. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website at pulselci.com or any of our services.

2. Information We Collect

Free report requests: When you request a free report we collect your name, work email, business name, city, state, and up to three competitor names you provide.

Subscriptions: When you start a paid subscription we collect your name, phone number, email address, business name, city, state, and the competitors you want tracked. Payment is handled by Stripe — we do not store your card number.

Information collected automatically:

  • Basic web analytics (pages visited, referral source) to understand how people find and use our site
  • IP address and browser type for security and performance purposes

Information from third parties:

  • Publicly available business and review data from Google Maps and Google Reviews, used solely to generate your competitive report

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and deliver your competitive intelligence report
  • To send follow-up emails related to your report or subscription
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To respond to questions or support requests you send us
  • To improve our services and understand usage patterns

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Email Communications

By requesting a free report or subscribing, you agree to receive transactional emails (your report, billing confirmations) and follow-up emails from Pulse LCI. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out of follow-up emails at any time without affecting report delivery or your subscription.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Google Maps Platform — to retrieve publicly available business and review data for reports
  • Stripe — to securely process subscription billing. Card data never touches our servers.
  • Email delivery provider — to reliably send reports and follow-ups to your inbox
  • Cloud hosting provider — to host our API and store data securely

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account or subscription is active, and for a reasonable period afterward for business and legal purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at craig@pulselci.com.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls on our database. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable steps to protect your data.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business owners and are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page and update the "Last updated" date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data? Email us at craig@pulselci.com.
